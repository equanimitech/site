## Principle write-ups belong to Torchbearer

Each of the nine principles deserves a fuller treatment than just name + design test. That's Torchbearer's job right now, not the landing page's.

When Torchbearer ships, the landing's `#framework` section can link each principle's name to its Torchbearer entry. Until then, the page shows name + test only; no broken link.

## North star: everything must earn its place

A standing rule for the landing site. Any section, sentence, or link that can be cut without losing the lab's voice or the visitor's reason to write to the door should be cut. The cleaner the page, the more the framework speaks.
